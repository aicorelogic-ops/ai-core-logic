The Silent Profit Killer

Your dispatchers are drowning. Every single day, your profit is leaking out of your business in 15-minute increments. It happens every time a dispatcher has to manually type an address, chase a BOL, or answer a "where is my load" call at 2 AM.

The industry average for manual data entry errors is 3.8%. In logistics, a 3.8% error rate on a $5,000 load is a disaster waiting to happen.
